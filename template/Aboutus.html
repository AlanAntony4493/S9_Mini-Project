Mission and Goals:

Since its inception, SMYM Mukkottathara has been guided by a noble mission: to create future leaders deeply grounded in their faith, culture, and values. This mission is reflected in the various programs and activities the organization has undertaken over the years.

About SMYM Mukkottuthara

Established in 2002, SMYM Mukkottuthara is dedicated to serving society and promoting the spiritual and cultural development of youth in the region.

BACKGROUND

SMYM Mukkottuthara is a vibrant and dynamic youth organization operating under the auspices of the Syro Malabar Church, specifically in the Kanjirappally Diocese. Established in 2002, SMYM Mukkottuthara is dedicated to serving society and promoting the spiritual and cultural development of youth in the region.

ACHIEVEMENTS

SMYM Mukkoottuthara Unit has consistently demonstrated excellence in the Kanjirappally Diocese, being recognized as the best unit among the 147 units three times. Notably, the unit achieved the title of Utsav Champions at the diocesan level competition, showcasing their prowess in various events. Additionally, they secured the Runners Up position in the Utsav-2021 and Utsav 2022 Foronae level competitions, further highlighting their commitment to success and achievement in diverse activities within the diocese.
